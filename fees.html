Important Notes

  • All fees are subject to change annually.
  • Fees do not include textbooks, which are purchased separately.
  • A non-refundable registration fee of ₦20,000 applies to new students.
  • Sibling discount of 10% available for second child and subsequent children
  • Payment can be made via bank transfer, online payment, or at the school's bursar's office
